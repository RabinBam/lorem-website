Postive Impact

Technology in watch has helped the seller tremendously as they are not limited to a particular region but entire world as they can import and export. It has also streamlined the process of design and manufacturing as before it required numerous trial and error to create a perfect design of watch but due to technology one can create a design virtual and simulate all the materials also can see how they react to particular condition.

Watches is able to gather more data and is able to connect seamlessly with smartphones, providing notifications, call handling, and app integration, improving productivity.

Smart watch with the metric to measure heart beats, oxygen level and many other metrics helps in the constant monitoring which at the end can end up in saving the life of people. 1
