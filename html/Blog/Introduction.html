Introduction

Technology is the fruit of countless trial and error where we have come from bulky computers to the microcomputer which have revolutionized the various fields. Watch industry is no exception to this revolution. The development in watch industry has created watch not just a mere device to look time at but a device which is comparable to hand held computers. Furthermore, the looks of the watch have also been quite heavily influenced from various kind of involvement of technology.

Some watch has not only became the symbol of royalty but it has also became Heirloom in many cases. Due to increase in the demand of the luxury watches its also treated as investment as luxury watches can be used to diversify the investment portfolio, and appreciation for fine watch-making 1

The technology has made it easy to buy and sell watches around the world due to which it had led to online auction for many of the antique watches. In 2017, the online distribution format was estimated to account for only 9% of the total luxury goods market value. This is predicted to increase to 25% by 2025 2 .

Which shows the radical shift of consumer behavior of purchasing the watches online as the technology evolves rapidly. Which is why many old companies have shifted their business online to adapt to this evolving market. As of 2025, there are approximately 30 major luxury watch brands recognized for their craftsmanship, heritage, and investment value 3 .
